Explanation of Relationships

1.Users → Posts: A user can create multiple posts (1:N).

2.Users → Likes: A user can like multiple posts (1:N).

3.Posts → Likes: A post can have many likes (1:N).

4.Posts → Post_Categories: Many-to-many relationship between posts and categories.

5.Categories → Post_Categories: Many-to-many relationship; a category can have multiple posts.
